Vector Rift

Build unknown

Objective

Defend the outer rim. Rotate across the lanes, shoot threats before they reach you, and survive as the arena changes shape.

Controls

Left/A and Right/D rotate. Space fires. Shift spends a full special meter. P pauses.

Enhancements

Evolving arenas change the lane rules by wave. Combo weapons reward clean kill streaks with higher scores and a charged lane-clearing special.

Scoring

Every hit adds score. With combo weapons enabled, kill streaks increase score and charge the special meter faster.
